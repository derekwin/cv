About Me

I'm Jinyao, a Master's student at the Changchun University of Science and Technology(CUST), supervised by Prof Hui Qi, majoring in Computer Science and Technology. My research interests include, but are hardly limited to:

- Internet protocols, particularly Transmission protocol Congestion control algorithm

- Reinforcemnt Learning, Deep Learning

My research focus on solving the Congestion control problem in weak network environment using Reinforcement Learning, such as Satellite Network, Transoceanic network. I received my bachelor degree from CUST as well, majoring in Electronic Science and Technology. Before my masters, I was an independent full stack developer (django, fastapi, vue, flutter) and web security CTFer.
